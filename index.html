About Golden Scheme Africa

Golden Scheme Africa (GSA) is a pioneering organization dedicated to transforming Africa through innovative solutions in key sectors that drive sustainable development. Founded in 2020, we have grown to become a leading force in empowering communities across the continent.

Our Approach

We combine local knowledge with global expertise to create solutions that are culturally relevant, technologically advanced, and economically viable. Our holistic approach ensures that every project we undertake creates lasting impact.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower African communities through innovative, sustainable solutions that harness our agricultural potential, technological capabilities, and creative talents while preserving our cultural heritage.

Our Vision

To be the leading catalyst for Africa's transformation, where every individual has access to opportunities that enable them to thrive while contributing to the continent's sustainable development.

Our Strategic Goals

1

Food Security

Increase agricultural productivity by 40% in our target communities through sustainable farming techniques and technology adoption.

2

Digital Inclusion

Provide digital skills training to 10,000 youth annually and support 500 tech startups across Africa by 2025.

3

Cultural Preservation

Document and promote 100+ indigenous art forms and support 200+ African artists in reaching global markets.

4

Sustainable Fashion

Establish Africa's first eco-friendly fashion hub that connects designers with sustainable materials and global buyers.

5

Economic Empowerment

Create 5,000 direct jobs and 15,000 indirect jobs through our various initiatives by 2026.
